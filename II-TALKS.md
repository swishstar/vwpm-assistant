## Naming Conventions

**Ticket products (WooCommerce):**

> `TICKET – [Talk Title] at [Venue Abbreviation] ([M/D])`

Examples:

- `TICKET – Money Matters at UUFVB (3/20)`
- `TICKET – Summoning Your Supernatural Self at SOD (2/27)`

**Recording products (WooCommerce):**

> `Recording – II TALKS: "[Talk Title]"`

- One product per talk title — all venue recordings are bundled as audio files in the same product.
- Anyone who attended in person receives a 100%-off coupon code specific to that recording product.

**Participant tags (Mailchimp):**

> `YYYY-MM-DD [VENUE] IITalks [TalkSlug] Participants`

Examples:

- `2026-03-20 UUFVB IITalks MoneyMatters Participants`
- `2026-02-27 SOD IITalks SYSS Participants`
