# Email Send Checklist

Use before **send**, **schedule**, or **go live** on a Mailchimp campaign.

## Content

- [ ] Subject line and preview text are set and match your voice
- [ ] Body copy is proofread (links, dates, names, venue)
- [ ] All links work and go to the right URLs
- [ ] CTAs are clear and match the goal of the send

## Audience

- [ ] Correct list or segment is selected
- [ ] Tags / `IITalk` (or other tags) are applied as intended
- [ ] Exclusion rules (if any) are correct so you do not email the wrong people

## Technical

- [ ] From name and reply-to are correct
- [ ] Test email sent and checked on phone + desktop (if you use tests)

## Timing

- [ ] Send time or schedule matches your plan (timezone, date)

## Compliance

- [ ] Physical mailing address and unsubscribe appear as required by Mailchimp/your policy

When everything above is done — or you accept the risk — type **GREEN LIGHT** to proceed.
